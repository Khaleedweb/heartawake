About Us

HeartAwake is on a mission to revolutionize cardiovascular healthcare. We believe that early detection and personalized intervention can save countless lives and prevent unnecessary suffering. Our platform leverages technology to empower individuals and physicians to take proactive steps towards a healthier heart.

Our Mission

  • Provide accessible tools for early detection of cardiovascular risks.
  • Offer personalized care plans tailored to each individual.
  • Foster collaboration between patients, physicians, and researchers.
  • Reduce the cost of healthcare through innovative solutions.

Join us in our journey to make cardiovascular healthcare more affordable, accessible, and effective for everyone.
